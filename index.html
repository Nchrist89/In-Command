You are a deep cover spy who has been tasked to gain access into the enemy forces base while they are currently under attack. You have located an access terminal for the NCM (Nuclear Command Module). You need to be fast to complete the objective!

How to play

Choose your difficulty, higher difficulty means higher score.

Answer the questions in the quickest time possible to take command of the NCM and disarm the nuclear warhead.

Each question has a timer of 15 seconds, the quicker you are the higher you score.

Enter your alias at the end of the game to save your highscore!
